## 测试3：口语

### Task 1：Work from home vs. office
**用户原文（语音输入）：**
> I prefer to work from home alone. I have three reasons. The first, I'm a multitask type of person, I really love doing several things together so I need a very large space prepared for me to work. A second, I don't like a small fixed space to work because I have to think a lot, so I really love go get around at a house, it can help me thinking. And the third is, I need another time different from others because I sleep late and get up late so um my work time is different from others, so I want to work alone because it can make me more efficiency and more and do better job.

**点评**：
- ✅ 结构清晰，三个理由
- ✅ 能持续输出，无长时间卡壳
- ❌ 语法错误："love go get around" → "love walking around", "more efficiency" → "more efficient"

### Task 2：Remove vending machines
**用户原文（语音输入）：**
> No, I don't think it's a good idea because choose what to eat is everyone's choice. You can tell them the vending machines, the food from many vending machines is not very healthier. You can tell them, you can teach them, but you can't bend them, you don't have the right to remove.

**点评**：
- ✅ 论点锐利，"you can teach them but you can't ban them"
- ✅ 逻辑完整，更自信
- ❌ 语法："choose what to eat is" → "choosing...", "not very healthier" → "not very healthy"

### 诊断
- 口语输出通道比写作通畅得多
- 结构组织能力好（有意识地分点论述）
- 主要问题：语法细节和用词精确度
- **预估分数：20-22**

## 关键发现

1. **理解力完好，输出力退化**：读得懂、听得明白方向没问题，但写和说跟不上
2. **口语 > 写作**：口头表达通道比书面通道通畅，这很关键——说明语言系统还在，只是书写端锈蚀更严重
3. **结构化思维强**：回答问题自觉分点，这是托福高分的天然优势
4. **语法不是不会，是生疏**：错误都是基础语法（主语it、动名词、比较级），不是不懂而是反应慢

## 优先级排序

1. 🔴 **写作**（差距最大，但AI批改提分最快）
2. 🔴 **听力**（不确定性最大，需尽快做真题确认）
3. 🟡 **阅读**（底子好，限时刷题+细节训练）
4. 🟢 **口语**（差距最小，模板+语法修正即可）
